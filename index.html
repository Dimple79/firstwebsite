How to be more productive by working less?

The more you work the more your brain is able to process information. But Productivity isn't just about getting things done. It doesn't depend upon on the number of working hours you spend in a day. It's about being productive and not wasting time on useless tasks that don't add value to your life. The key is to make sure you're doing what makes you happy. And that means making sure you're doing things that bring joy to your life. The idea is just to do smart work and improving your efficiency rather than being indulging in working all day long. The best way to achieve this is by focussing on one thing at a time. You can do this by taking breaks from your daily routine, or even just going for a walk around. It refreshes your mind and gives you a chance to think outside the box.
